What Are Binary Options Scams?

Binary options are a simple and highly popular form of daily trading that can be rather lucrative, but it also carries considerable risks. Scammers exploit this and they set up fake brokers and thieving trading platforms that promote the possibility of binary options trading.

Usually, they lure investors with excellent trading conditions, flashy sign-up bonuses, unreasonable ROI, and auto-trading bots that end up being malware.

HOW TO SPOT BINARY OPTIONS SCAM BROKERS

Fraudulent brokers are fake trading platforms that practice social engineering and use dirty tricks to extort your money. Sometimes it is difficult to discern a fake broker from a legitimate one. For this reason, we provide you with a list of possible warning signs:

  • They Don’t Own A License
  • They Do Not Provide Transparency About Fees
  • They Have A Fake Address Or Telephone Number
  • They Do Not Allow A Variety Of Platforms And Payment Options
  • They Are Evasive And Don’t Give Clear Information
